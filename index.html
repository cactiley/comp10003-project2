Welcome to the Unofficial Exploding Kittens Website!

Created by a small team of card game enthusiasts, this site aims to introduce and explain the wildly popular card game Exploding Kittens. Created by The Oatmeal in 2015, this game quickly became a hit and has rapidly grown, releasing expansions, a mobile version and a Netflix TV series. This game involves careful strategy, focus… and just *some* targeted attacks, making it a crowd-pleaser at any game night. This website explains a bit of the history of Exploding Kittens, as well as a foolproof (we hope) guide to the game. We've also included some of our favourite tips and tricks to ensure success in avoiding an explosion-related death, a curated list of the best cards to hold on to and a quiz to test your knowledge on the game.
