Tips and Tricks

These are the tips and tricks that you will definetly need when you play Exploding Kittens! They will guide you to play smarter and get a better chance at victory

1. Exploding Kittens Incoming!

The chance of drawing an Exploding Kitten increases as gameply continues - make sure to hoard cards and attack other players towards the end.

2. Sabatoging

You can choose a specific position and count cards when replacing them in the deck - placing them a few rounds ahead allows you to have a turn and sabotage your victim.

3. Timing to Steal

Stealing later into the game, especially when players have a few cards allows a higher chance of stealing a valuable card such as a defuse - count and track defuse cards!



4. Skip Later

When there are no more defuse cards in play, skips become just as valuable, including cards that allow you to skip turns! Do not waste these early on.

5. Cat Cards Overkill

Once the deck gets low, other players will likely be saving these valuable cards - play the majority of your cat cards at this time to strengthen your hand and weaken theirs.

6. "It's not going to change anything"

Do not look into the future if there is no way to change your fate - you will both waste cards and be disappointed simultaneously.
